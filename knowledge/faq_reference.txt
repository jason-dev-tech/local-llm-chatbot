FAQ Reference

Q: Why do trust and citations matter in a RAG system?
A: Citations show which source supported the answer, which helps users check whether the response is grounded.

Q: What role does metadata play?
A: Metadata keeps track of fields such as filename, source, and chunk index so the system can attribute retrieved content clearly.

Q: Why is attribution useful?
A: Attribution helps debugging, improves transparency, and makes it easier to confirm where a fact came from.
